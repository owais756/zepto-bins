# Zepto-Bins

This website was developed at the request of a Zepto franchise owner to address a recurring operational challenge within the store.

During order handovers, packers and delivery riders were required to scan QR codes and barcodes attached to Zepto bins. Over time, these physical labels frequently became damaged, faded, torn, or difficult to scan, resulting in delays and inefficiencies during the handover process.

To solve this issue, I proposed a digital alternative that would make bin identification and scanning more reliable and accessible. As a result, Zepto-Bins was created as an online platform where bin codes can be accessed digitally without depending on physical QR code or barcode stickers.

By eliminating the risk of damaged labels, the platform streamlines the handover process, improves operational efficiency, reduces scanning errors, and provides a smoother experience for both packers and delivery riders.
